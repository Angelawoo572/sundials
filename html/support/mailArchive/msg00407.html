Hi Andreas,

Let me add a little to Radu's reply.

For a nonsingular 2x2 matrix, the inverse is trivial to express
explicitly.  If it is constant, you can economize further by saving
the coefficients of the inverse.  So it is likely that for this
problem, CVODE will be more efficient that IDA, which has extra
overhead to deal with the implicitness of the problem form.  But if
speed is a big concern, it might be enlightening to try both and see.

-Alan
