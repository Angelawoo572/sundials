It is a big job and bigger by the time you asm optimize sections. A new version of an ODE solver (that is, the same
solver) is usually never radically different from it predecessor (often just support routines but hardly ever the core), and
so revisions are not difficult. That is, if you know the solver.

What proves to be more viable right now is that I have selected portions already in Delphi/asm that are linked as
.obj files -- critical sections in other words. I don't see the porting the whole thing as an option, though, as I doubt
any speedup would be worth it.
