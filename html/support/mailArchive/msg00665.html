This probably less easily obtainable reference has also some a treatment
on preconditioners:

G.Meurant
Computer Solution of Large Linear Systems
Studies in mathematics and its applications, Vol 28.
Elsevier 1999

However, I am also interested in a 'preconditioning for chemists'
reference, as it seems to me to be a practical and experience based
task. I briefly tried to look for Alan's comments on publications
directly associated with the solvers on this but I cannot find anything
which tells how to do preconditioning for reactive flow problems.
Probably you take the block-by-block inverse of the decoupled kinetic
models, as the kinetics dominate the transport equations.

Maarten Nauta
