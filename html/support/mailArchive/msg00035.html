Andreas,

Calling CVode in CV_ONE_STEP mode only adds the overhead of additional function calls. Otherwise, CVODE does the exact same amount of work in both CV_NORMAL and CV_ONE_STEP modes (see my reply to Jordan earlier today).
On the other hand, if you know beforehand the time(s) at which you'd like to change the tolerances, you could call CVode in CV_NORMAL mode between these times.

Yes, CVodeSetTolerances will be provided in future releases (as the only 
mechanism for changing tolerances).

--Radu
