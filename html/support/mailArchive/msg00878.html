You need to see the SUNDIALS Support page, and the FAQ
   How do I handle a data-defined function within the RHS function?

In order to get reasonable efficiency out of CVODE, you need to
construct a smooth interpolating function for the forcing terms,
allowing CVODE to call up their values at arbitrary times.  A
piecewise linear interpolant may be sufficient.  A piecewise constant
one should not be used, since it would cause unneeded extra costs at
the break points.  No amount of playing with tolerances can compensate
for the lack of a good interpolant.

-Alan H.
