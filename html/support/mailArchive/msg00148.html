From Alan Hindmarsh <alanh@llnl.gov>
Date Wed, 19 Oct 2005 12:56:37 -0700 (PDT) 
cj is a scalar set within IDA.  It is documented under the various Jacobian
routines as follows (taken from the IDA User Guide):

  \item[c\_j] is the scalar in the system Jacobian, proportional to the inverse
    of the step size ($\alpha$ in Eq. (\ref{e:DAE_Jacobian}) ).

The equation referenced is Eq. (3.5) in Section 3.

-Alan H.
