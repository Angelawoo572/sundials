Hi Joerg,

which output are you talking about? File or screen? In case you are writing to 
file be aware of certain caching operations running in the background - either 
already implemented in your standard lib (e.g. in case of std::fstream) or as 
part of the OS. Maybe for your shorter runtimes the output fits into the 
buffer, but for the longer ones, the buffer needs to be written periodically, 
causing some delays...
