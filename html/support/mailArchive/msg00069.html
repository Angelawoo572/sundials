Hello,

I am putting together a presentation on SUNDIALS and I realized that it would be nice to have a list of applications of SUNDIALS outside our group. So, if you can share some of your results, I'd really appreciate getting a note from you with a couple of lines describing what you use it for, maybe a picture or two, and (if available) a link to your website.

For this presentation I am particularly interested in sensitivity analysis applications, but I'd like to hear about any kind of application, as it would also be nice to set-up a 'User Gallery' page on the SUNDIALS website.

Also, if you are aware of any, please let me know of other packages that 
incorporate or make use of the SUNDIALS solvers.
