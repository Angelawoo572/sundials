Andreas Nicolai wrote:
Hi Mukesh,

this doesn't sound like a UNIX compilation problem, rather a problem with different implementations of the standard math functions on different plattforms and compilers, especially since you mentioned the compiler options. It seems that turning on optimized compilation causes the problem. I would suggest that you try safe-math flags (if they exist for your compiler) or check whether you might be able to disable certain math optimizations when you compile under unix.
