There is LOTS of information about preconditioners for reaction-transport
PDE systems in our two papers
      P. N. Brown and A. C. Hindmarsh, "Reduced Storage Matrix Methods
     in Stiff ODE Systems," J. Appl. Math. & Comp., 31 (1989), pp.40-91.
     Also LLNL report UCRL-95088, Rev. 1, June 1987.
and
   . G. D. Byrne, "Pragmatic Experiments with Krylov Methods in the
     Stiff ODE Setting," Computational Ordinary Differential Equations,
     J. Cash and I. Gladwell, eds., Oxford Univ. Press, Oxford, 1992,
     pp. 323-356.
The 'reaction-only' case is the first of several discussed.

-Alan H.
