Andreas,

Please give a more complete description of your problem. You have never mentioned discontinuities in your problem until now. What exactly do you mean by "boundary conditions"? Are these discontinuities just rapid changes in the solution that the model itself generates or are they jumps/changes (in the solution or the RHS) that you incorporate?

If the former, that could explain smaller time steps.

If the latter, we could not have guessed that since your code snippet did not show any reinitialization of the solver (see the "Usage notes" on the website!). If this is the case, what type of discontinuity do you have (in the solution itself or in the RHS) and how do you incorporate these discontinuities?

Note that, given the presence of discontinuities, the use of CVodeDky that I 
mentioned in my previous post may not work as stated.

--Radu
