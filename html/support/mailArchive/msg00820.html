Hi all,

first of all I'd like to compliment the Sundials developpers. I was
looking for a DAE-Integrator with a Matlab interface and decided to go
for sundials. The installation of SundialsTB was extremely (and really sprisingly)
easy and the solver works great. My question is: Is there any estimate on when idas will be out and once
it is out, will the idas MEX-interface work right away?

I know that it is typically hard to estimate the release date for a
software package, however, a rough estimate would help.

Thanks

Claas


Hi Claas,
The idas module in sundialsTB currently interfaces to the IDA solver in SUNDIALS and, as such, is exactly what you are looking for. (I've opted for the name idas so that, when the sensitivity-capable solver in SUNDIALS will be available, this Matlab module will also provide SA. Since IDAS will be a superset of IDA, the future idas module in sundialsTB will be backward compatible.)
--Radu
