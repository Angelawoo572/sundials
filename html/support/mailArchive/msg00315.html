Radu Serban <serban1@llnl.gov> wrote:
The proper way to implement this is, I think, by adding an additional variable (and equation) to
your ODE system that would compute the desired quadrature. Take a look at the CVODES user guide and
search for 'pure quadrature integration' to see how that can be done.

What you have now is a very inaccurate approximation (moreover, the code snippet below does not seem
right: the right term in your "! if" statement is something like flux(y(t))*(t-ti) with ti>t)

--Radu
