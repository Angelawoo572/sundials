Hi Ramis,

Every single SUNDIALS solver comes with several examples (using either the serial or parallel NVECTOR module) which can be found under sundials/solver/examples_ser and sundials/solver/examples_par, respectively (where solver is one of cvode, cvodes, ida, kinsol). Also, some of the examples are explained in more detail in the companion **_examples.pdf document (available online or under sundials/solver/doc).

Hope that this can get you started.
