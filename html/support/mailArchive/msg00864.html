Chris-

You evidently have a problem for which unpreconditioned Krylov iteration
is no good.  The DENSE solver uses all of the Jacobian.  SPGMR with no
preconditioner uses none of it.  If you can identify a dominant part
of the Jacobian and use it to generate and solve a preconditioner
(more efficiently than a dense solve), that will probably be better
than either of the other choices.

-Alan H.
